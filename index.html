About Us

One of the leading real estate development company in India

Founded in 1995, Sobha Limited housing is a Indian Multinational construction company that has been primarily involved in construction and developmental activities of residential and commercial nature. The headquarters of the company stand located in Bengaluru, Karnataka. Apart from their focus on construction they are also involved in property management and sales.

The key markets of the company include cities of Bangalore, Kochi, Gurgaon, Chennai, Pune, and Mysore. Internationally, the firm has its operations mainly in Dubai and Oman.
