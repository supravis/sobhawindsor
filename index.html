About Us

One of the leading real estate development company in India

SOBHA Limited is an Indian multinational real estate developer headquartered in Bangalore, India. Which works in the business of construction, development, sale, management and operation, townships, housing projects, commercial premises and other related activities.

SOBHA Limited was founded in 1995 by Mr. P.N.C. Menon. Since inception, as of December 31, 2019 SOBHA has delivered real estate projects and contractual projects covering about 106.80 million square feet of area. The Company currently has ongoing real estate projects aggregating to 40.94 million square feet of developable area and 28.34 million square feet of saleable area, and ongoing contractual projects aggregating to 10.51 million square feet under various stage of construction. The Company has a real estate presence in 10 cities, viz. Bangalore, Gurgaon, Chennai, Pune, Coimbatore, Thrissur, Calicut, Cochin, Gujarat (Gift City) and Mysore. Overall, SOBHA has footprint in 27 cities and 14 states across India. The company has also executed projects for some of India's corporate houses, including Infosys, WIPRO, HCL, Dell, Bosch, Biocon, Taj Group of Hotels and ITC Hotels.
